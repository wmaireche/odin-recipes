Marinated chicken sandwich recipe

This recipe is easy to prepare, economic and full of flavor

Indredients

  • Bread
  • 100 grames of chicken
  • 1 Potatoe
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1/2 Lemon
  • Salt, Pepper, Paprika, Mustard

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 210°C (Th. 7).
  2. Serve the beef sandwiches hot, accompanied by very cold coleslaw.
  3. Heat a frying pan with olive oil, brown the chopped onions for 5 minutes over moderate heat, add the minced beef and basil, cook for 1 minute over high heat, stirring, salt and pepper.
  4. Heat the baguettes for 1 to 2 minutes in the oven at 180°C, open them and brush the inside with strong mustard, garnish them with hot onion beef.
  5. Serve the beef sandwiches hot, accompanied by very cold coleslaw.
